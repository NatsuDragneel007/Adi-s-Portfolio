About Me → “The Knight”

In chess, every piece has its place — predictable, linear, constrained. But I’ve always connected with the Knight. It jumps over obstacles, moves in ways others can’t, and often becomes the most surprising piece on the board. That’s me. I adapt, I solve, I create unexpected solutions — whether in design, campaigns, or AI workflows. Alone, the knight is powerful. But when paired with another, it can control 16 blocks at once — a full 25% of the board. That’s how I see teamwork: multiplying impact. Beyond strategy, I’m simply someone who believes in listening carefully, observing deeply, and moving when the timing is right.
